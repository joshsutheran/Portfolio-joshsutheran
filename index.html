About Me

I'm a front end web developer, with over a year's worth of personal experience. I'm an incredibly determined developer, who has balanced self teaching with leading a Geography department in an inner city SE London school.

I have completed a sequenced curriculum, devised and supported by a network of experienced software engineers, that has equipped me with core front end development skills, alongside experience with some backend technologies. These include:

  • HTML 5
  • CSS and SaSS
  • JavaScript (ES6+)
  • Mongo DB
  • Modern React and Redux
  • Node JS
  • TypeScript
  • Express JS

Alongside my teaching career, I am entreprenurial, with experience of running multiple businesses. These experiences have provided me with search engine optimisation (SEO) skills, UI/UX design experience, alongside online marketing campaigns and advertisement.
